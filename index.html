Under 'National Language Translation Mission' project funded by MeitY, We organize a series of challenges related to Speech Technologies. Results of these challenges are published on the Leadership Board of the NPLT website. Challenges related to the Text to Speech Synthesis System, Automatic Speech Recognition System, and Lip-Syncing have already been organized.

Here is the list of areas in which we organize challenges:

  1. Automatic Speech Recognition
  2. Text-to-speech
  3. Lip-Sync
  4. Diarization
  5. Audio-visual syncing
  6. Prosody manipulation for conversational TTS
  7. Voice Conversion
  8. S2S System with lip-sync
  9. Speech-based applications
